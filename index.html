About

His Church, Our City is a city wide worship event that displays the value of church unity, community impact, and Gospel centrality.

This event was started by a group of local church pastors who have a heart and burden to see the city of Fort Mill transformed by the Gospel of Jesus Christ, and that we THE CHURCH are better together.


This is our 5th annual gathering - join us on Sunday, September 8th for an incredible evening of worship as churches from all over Fort Mill come together united by Jesus with a mission to make our city BETTER!

Event Details

Date: Sunday, September 8th

Time: 4pm-8pm

Location: Anne Springs Greenway Amphitheater (Dairy Barn Entrance) and Dairy Barn

Food Trucks: 4pm-5:30pm

Worship Service: 5:30pm-7pm



Anne Springs Close Greenway: Dairy Barn Entrance

The beautiful 2,100 acre greenway will be the host of our event. The Comporium Amphitheater provides the perfect setting for a great evening of fellowship and worship for the entire family. Dairy Barn Entrance Address: 291 Dairy Barn Lane, Fort Mill, SC 29715. For more information on the Anne Springs Close Greenway go to: https://www.ascgreenway.org/

Parking

Last year we completely filled up the onsite parking! Therefore, in addition to onsite parking, we will have shuttle buses going to and from Lifepointe Church which is only minutes away from the venue. In order to make room for elderly and families with small children onsite, we ask that if you are able, please park at Lifepointe Church (390 York Southern Rd, FM, 29715) and ride the shuttle to the venue.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this event free?

Yes!

What happens if it rains?

Depending on the severity of the rain the event would be cancelled. Please check your church's social media for rain out details (if applicable).

Will there be restrooms available?

Yes!

Should I bring my own chair or blanket?

Yes! The venue provides no seating so a chair or blanket would be perfect!

Will police officers and a medical team be present?

Absolutely! Your safety at this event is our top priority. We will have York County Sheriffs on site as well as a medical, first-aid tent.

What else should I bring?

Feel free to bring any yard games, football, sports equipment to enjoy with friends and family.
