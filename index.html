About

His Church, Our City is back for the third year in a row! This event was started by a group of local church pastors who have a heart and burden to see the city of Fort Mill transformed by the Gospel of Jesus Christ, and that we the “Church” are better together.

Join us this Labor Day weekend, Sunday, September 4th for an incredible evening of worship as churches from all over Fort Mill come together united by Jesus with a mission to make our city BETTER!

Event Details

Date: Sunday, September 4th

Time: 5pm-8pm

Location: Anne Springs Close Greenway- Dairy Barn Entrance

Food Trucks: 5pm-6:30pm

Worship Service: 6:30pm-8pm

Anne Springs Close Greenway: Dairy Barn Entrance

The beautiful 2,100 acre greenway will be the host of our event. The Comporium Amphitheater provides the perfect setting for a great evening of fellowship and worship for the entire family.


Dairy Barn Entrance Address: 291 Dairy Barn Lane Fort Mill, SC 29715.

For more information on the Anne Springs Close Greenway go to: https://www.ascgreenway.org/

Parking

All parking will be on site this year at the Dairy Barn Entrance.

Food Truck

Our Food Truck Row will be ready to serve and feed your family starting at 5pm!
