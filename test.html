About

His Church, Our City is a city wide worship event that displays the value of church unity, community impact, and Gospel centrality.

His Church, Our City is back for the third year in a row! This event was started by a group of local church pastors who have a heart and burden to see the city of Fort Mill transformed by the Gospel of Jesus Christ, and that we the “Church” are better together.

Join us this Labor Day weekend, Sunday, September 4th for an incredible evening of worship as churches from all over Fort Mill come together united by Jesus with a mission to make our city BETTER!

This Years Fundraiser

This year we are partnering with Isaiah 117 House York County. We will have tents set up where you can get information about our service project for this year and where families can register to be part of the Lemonade Stand Initiative to raise money for this incredible cause. This is what Isaiah 117 House is all about:


"When children are removed from their homes out of concern for their safety, they are usually brought to a child welfare services office to await placement. This wait can be a few hours to several days. These children often have nothing with them and are scared, lonely, hungry, and in dirty clothing.

Isaiah 117 provides a comforting home where these children instead can be brought to wait a place that is safe with friendly and loving volunteers who provide clean clothes, smiles, toys, and snuggly blankets. This space allows children to receive the comfort and care they need while child welfare staff can do the necessary paperwork and identify a good placement".


The mission of Isaiah 117 House is threefold:

  1. reduce trauma for children awaiting placement
  2. lighten the load for child welfare services
  3. ease the transition for foster families


To learn more about Isaiah 117 House click here: - https://isaiah117house.com


Frequently Asked Questions

Is this event free?

Yes!

What happens if it rains?

Depending on the severity of the rain the event would be cancelled. Please check your church's social media for rain out details (if applicable).

Will there be restrooms available?

Yes!

Should I bring my own chair or blanket?

Yes! The venue provides no seating so a chair or blanket would be perfect!

Will police officers and a medical team be present?

Absolutely! Your safety at this event is our top priority. We will have York County Sheriffs on site as well as a medical, first-aid tent.

What else should I bring?

Feel free to bring any yard games, football, sports equipment to enjoy with friends and family.
